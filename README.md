### About Me

- :man_technologist: Platform & Site Reliability Engineer at [Stakater](https://www.stakater.com), working on managed **OpenShift / Kubernetes** and **Cloud Orchestrator** — running private clouds for VMs and Kubernetes without VMware
- :cloud: Day-to-day I work with **Crossplane** (compositions, XRDs & functions), **KubeVirt** virtualization, **kcp** multi-tenancy, and **GitOps** with Argo CD
- :gear: I develop Kubernetes **operators and controllers** in Golang, and go deep on **backup & disaster recovery** — Velero / OADP, etcd & VM snapshots, and cross-cluster restore
- :globe_with_meridians: Cloud & platform infra across **AWS** (IAM, KMS, S3), **Ceph / OpenShift Data Foundation** storage, **secrets management** (HashiCorp Vault / OpenBao, External Secrets, KMS auto-unseal), and **SSO / OIDC & identity** (Keycloak, **Azure AD / Entra ID**, PIM)
- :arrows_counterclockwise: Virtualization & migration — **KubeVirt / OpenShift Virtualization** lifecycle and **VMware → OpenShift** migrations via MTV
- :seedling: Contributing across the cloud-native / DevOps open-source ecosystem, always looking for new challenges in the space
- :no_mobile_phones: Outside of work, most of my time goes to FPS games :video_game:, losing at chess :chess_pawn:, or supporting my favourite esports teams on Twitch :space_invader:
